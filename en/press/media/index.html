Media dossier

This page gathers public media coverage of the Gómez Aldaz case in the press, radio, television, magazines, news agencies and digital media.

The dossier preserves both active links and archived copies of articles that have been removed or are no longer publicly available, in order to maintain the documentary traceability of the media coverage.

The entries are organised by type of media and by media cycle: the 2025 court ruling, the 2026 documentary restitution and later interviews.
