My most personal interview

Published in El Observatorio no. 9 under the title “La batalla por mi identidad”.

In June 2026, El Observatorio, a free semiannual magazine produced by the Taller Prelaboral Pinoduero in Tudela de Duero, published an extensive interview about my personal and legal story.

The interview arose from a visit to the Taller Prelaboral Pinoduero, where I shared my story, presented my book and explained the process of searching for my origins, restoring my identity and obtaining judicial recognition of my biological filiation.

The piece covers the search for my origins, the encounter with my biological sister, the DNA test, the judicial recovery of my biological filiation and the harm caused by identity theft and fraudulent adoption.

It is one of the most complete and personal interviews I have given so far. In it, I explain not only the documentary and legal facts of the case, but also the intimate experience of the search, loneliness, psychological harm, lack of recognition and the emotional weight of having had to reconstruct my own identity.

The interview occupies pages 3 to 10 of issue no. 9 of the magazine and has special value because of its local, community-based and human dimension.
