Published work, critical archive and research

This page gathers the published, documentary and critical work of Olmo Gómez Aldaz on identity, filiation, adoption, stolen babies, democratic memory and documentary restitution.

The section organises books, testimonies, critical texts and documentary research linked to the recovery of birth identity, biological filiation, dual filiation and the denunciation of irregular adoption.
