Work > Research on the María Madre Association

This section brings together documentation and research on the María Madre Association of Bilbao, an entity founded in 1969 under ecclesiastical and administrative cover, linked to practices of abduction, transfer and irregular adoption of newborns during Francoism and the transition.

Alongside the documentary report on the association, it includes the preservation and analysis of AdoptadosBilbao.Blogspot.com, a pioneering space of public memory, denunciation and search by victims and relatives connected to this network.

IAMM – Report on the María Madre Association of Bilbao

Documentary, historical and legal report on the María Madre Association of Bilbao: its foundation, structure, Church-State cover, recorded activity and role in the network of newborn abduction and irregular adoptions.
