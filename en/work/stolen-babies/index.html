Research on stolen babies

This section brings together documentation, research and critical archive work on stolen babies, identity theft, disappearance of origins, filiation, irregular adoptions and public memory in Spain.

Within this general framework, it presents two works linked to the Maria Madre Association of Bilbao: the documentary report on the organisation and the report on AdoptadosBilbao.Blogspot.com, a historical blog created by victims and relatives connected to this network.

Both documents complement each other: the IAMM report reconstructs the institutional structure of Maria Madre; the AdoptadosBilbao report reconstructs the digital memory of the victims, their searches, testimonies and public denunciations.

IAMM – Report on the Maria Madre Association of Bilbao

Documentary, historical and legal report on the Maria Madre Association of Bilbao: its foundation, structure, Church-State cover, recorded activity and role in the network of newborn abduction and irregular adoptions.
